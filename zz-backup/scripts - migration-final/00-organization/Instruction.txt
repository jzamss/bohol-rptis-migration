ORGANIZATION
==============================

1. Add lguid and brgyid field to rptis.m_barangay.

  For LGU's not implemenenting RPTIS module, 
  execute "00-organization-new.sql"

  otherwise:

  execute "00-organization-existing.sql"

  In case of error, post to GC.


2. Login to ETRACS. Open Administration - Organization.
   Verify that Province, Municipality and Barangay are 
   correctly populated 

3. Mark Monitoring as "OK"


NOTE FOR PROVINCE: 
* Verify that municipalities and barangays are properly merge
